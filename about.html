Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010, Honey Shop started as a small family business with just 5 beehives in the countryside. Our founder, John Beekeeper, had a passion for pure, natural honey and wanted to share it with the world.

Our Process

Beekeeping

We maintain over 200 hives across organic farms, ensuring our bees have access to the purest nectar sources.

Harvesting

Our honey is harvested only twice a year to allow proper maturation and to not stress the bees.

Processing

We use cold extraction methods to preserve all the natural enzymes and nutrients in the honey.

Packaging

All our honey is packaged in glass jars to maintain purity and prevent chemical leaching.

Our Products

  • Raw, unfiltered honey
  • Organic honey varieties (Wildflower, Clover, Orange Blossom)
  • Honey-based skincare products
  • Specialty infused honeys (Cinnamon, Ginger, Chili)
  • Beeswax candles and products
